Getting Started

What is Rytuals?
Rytuals is an AI-powered spiritual guidance app that provides personalized astrology readings, tarot consultations, oracle conversations, and spiritual journaling. We combine ancient wisdom with modern technology to offer authentic cosmic insights tailored to your unique astrological profile.
How do I create an account?
You can sign up using:
  • Apple Sign-In: One-tap signup with your Apple ID
  • Google Sign-In: Quick signup with your Google account
  • Email: Create an account with your email and password
After signing up, you'll complete a brief onboarding process to set up your cosmic profile.
What information do I need to provide during onboarding?
To provide accurate astrological guidance, we collect:
  • Birth date (required) - for zodiac sign calculation
  • Birth time (optional) - for precise chart readings
  • Birth location (optional) - for location-based calculations
  • Spiritual interests - to personalize your experience
All information is securely stored and used only to enhance your spiritual guidance.
Is Rytuals free to use?
Yes! Rytuals offers both free and premium features:
  • Free: Daily horoscopes, limited oracle conversations (3/day), basic tarot readings, and 5 journal entries per month
  • Premium: Unlimited access to all features, advanced birth chart analysis, detailed compatibility reports, and ad-free experience
Quick Start Tip: After creating your account, visit the Today tab for your personalized daily horoscope, then explore the Discover tab to try tarot readings and oracle conversations!

How do I get a tarot reading?
  1. Go to the Discover tab
  2. Select "Tarot & Oracle"
  3. Tap "Draw Cards" to receive your reading
  4. Your AI-powered interpretation will be personalized based on your astrological profile
Note: Premium users get unlimited readings, while free users have daily limits.
How does the Oracle conversation work?
The Oracle feature provides conversational spiritual guidance:
  • Ask questions about love, career, spiritual growth, or life decisions
  • Receive personalized responses based on your astrological profile
  • Continue the conversation for deeper insights
  • Free users get 3 questions per day, premium users get unlimited access
How accurate are the readings?
Our readings combine traditional astrological wisdom with AI technology for personalized insights. While we strive for meaningful guidance, please remember:
  • All readings are for entertainment and self-reflection purposes
  • Use your own judgment and intuition when interpreting guidance
  • Readings should not replace professional advice for important decisions
  • The AI learns from your preferences to provide more relevant insights over time
Can I save my readings?
Yes! All your readings are automatically saved in your account:
  • Recent readings appear in your profile history
  • Journal entries are saved with timestamps
  • Oracle conversations are preserved for future reference
  • You can review past insights anytime in your profile

Subscriptions & Billing

What premium plans are available?
We offer two premium subscription options:
  • Monthly: $6.99 per month
  • Annual: $59.99 per year (save $24 - equivalent to $4.17/month)
Both plans include unlimited access to all premium features.
What's included in premium?
Premium subscription includes:
  • Unlimited oracle conversations - ask as many questions as you want
  • Unlimited tarot readings - draw cards whenever inspiration strikes
  • Unlimited journal entries - record all your spiritual experiences
  • Advanced birth chart features - detailed planetary analysis
  • Compatibility reports - explore relationships with others
  • Priority cosmic insights - enhanced personalization
  • Ad-free experience - uninterrupted spiritual guidance
How do I subscribe to premium?
  1. Go to your Profile tab
  2. Tap "Upgrade to Premium"
  3. Choose your preferred plan (Monthly or Annual)
  4. Complete purchase through Apple's secure payment system
  5. Premium features activate immediately
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Select "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "Rytuals" and tap it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
Your premium features will continue until the end of your current billing period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple according to their App Store policies:
  • Contact Apple Support directly for refund requests
  • You can also request refunds through your Apple ID account
  • Refund eligibility depends on Apple's terms and conditions
  • We recommend trying the free version first to ensure Rytuals meets your needs
Do you offer free trials?
While we don't currently offer a traditional free trial, our free tier provides substantial access to experience Rytuals:
  • Daily personalized horoscopes
  • 3 oracle questions per day
  • Basic tarot readings
  • 5 journal entries per month
This allows you to fully evaluate the app before deciding on premium.

Technical Support

What devices does Rytuals support?
Rytuals is designed for iOS devices:
  • Minimum iOS version: iOS 18.5
  • Compatible devices: iPhone and iPad
  • Optimized for: Latest iPhone models with full-screen support
  • Internet required: Yes, for AI-powered features and cloud sync
The app won't load or is crashing. What should I do?
Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Force quit and restart the app
  2. Check your internet connection - Rytuals requires internet for AI features
  3. Update the app to the latest version from the App Store
  4. Restart your device to clear temporary issues
  5. Free up storage space if your device is running low
If problems persist, contact our support team.
I'm having issues with sign-in. What can I do?
Sign-in troubleshooting:
  • Apple Sign-In: Ensure you're signed into iCloud in Settings
  • Google Sign-In: Check that you're signed into your Google account in Safari
  • Email sign-in: Verify your email and password are correct
  • Network issues: Try switching between WiFi and cellular data
  • Clear cache: Delete and reinstall the app if necessary
Why aren't my readings generating?
If readings aren't generating properly:
  • Check internet connection - AI features require stable internet
  • Verify account setup - ensure your birth data is complete
  • Free tier limits - check if you've reached daily limits
  • Server status - rarely, our AI service may be temporarily unavailable
  • App update - ensure you have the latest version
The app will show fallback content if AI services are temporarily unavailable.
How do I update my birth information?
To modify your astrological profile:
  1. Go to your Profile tab
  2. Tap "Edit Profile"
  3. Update your birth date, time, or location
  4. Save changes - your readings will be updated accordingly
Note: Changing birth data will affect future readings and astrological calculations.
How do I delete my account?
To permanently delete your account and all data:
  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion
Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your readings, journal entries, and profile data will be permanently deleted.
